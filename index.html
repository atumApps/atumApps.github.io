About us

atum Apps is an indie app (mostly games) development studio based in Exeter, UK. We are a small team, dedicated to creating new and engaging app experiences.

Like all the best things, atum Apps was formed in a third floor apartment in Paris, France in January 2021. We moved to our current home in Exeter in summer 2022.

All of our apps aim to provide a clean, polished, and unique experience. If you like our work, don't hesitate to leave a review and some stars - they go a long way!

Our apps are written natively in Swift for iOS. If you'd like to see Android versions, let us know.

For any/all enquiries, please get in touch.

Privacy Policy

In Tiny Evolution, Google Mobile Ads may use the advertising ID of your device to serve targeted ads. Tiny Evolution uses Google analytics to monitor and improve the app experience. Further details of how your personal data is handled through Google Analytics can be found here.

All other apps collect no personal data at all and do not transmit any logging data back to the developers (us).

All our apps require no device permissions. In order to access optional Game Center achievements, users must login with their Game Center account, which is handled by Apple.

In order to purchase optional additional content, users must complete a payment transaction, which is handled by Apple.
